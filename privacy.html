Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BolBol Learning ("we", "us", or "our") manages information in connection with the BolBol mobile application (the "App") designed for children under 6.

We prioritize the privacy and safety of children above all else. The App is fully built as an offline learning platform that is safe, ad-free, and respectful of user confidentiality.

1. No Personal Data Collection

The App does not collect, record, transmit, or share any personal information. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account usernames.
  • Physical addresses or precise geographical locations.
  • Device IDs, IP addresses, advertising identifiers, or browser logs.
  • Audio inputs or painting inputs (all tracing and audio playback happens completely locally in the device's volatile memory and is never saved or transmitted).

2. COPPA Compliance (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

Because BolBol is designed for young children under the age of 6, we are fully committed to protecting their privacy under COPPA. Since the App is entirely ad-free and collects absolutely no data, we are in absolute alignment with children's safety regulations.

We do not contact children directly, we do not require parental sign-ins to access core features, and we prevent kids from opening custom URLs or support mail links via our secure Parental Gate.

3. Camera, Audio, & Storage Permissions

The App may request local system permissions to operate certain elements:

  • Audio Playback: The App uses the device's sound channels to play pre-recorded learning words and ElevenLabs synthesized stories. It does not record audio from the microphone.
  • Local Storage: Temporary assets (like downloaded story audio or custom avatar profiles) may be cached locally on the device's secure internal storage. They are never exported or uploaded.

4. No Third-Party Advertisements

We believe that learning spaces should be distraction-free. BolBol features zero third-party ads (no banners, no interstitials, no promotional video scripts). Your child will never be redirected to external websites.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the App is completely offline, we recommend reviewing this web page periodically for any modifications. All changes are effective immediately upon posting.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@bolbollearning.com
