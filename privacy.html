Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BolBol Learning ("we", "us", or "our") manages information in connection with the BolBol mobile application (the "App") designed for children under 6.

We prioritize the privacy and safety of children above all else. The App is fully built as an offline learning platform that is safe, private, and respectful of user confidentiality.

1. Information We Collect and Process

To provide a personalized learning experience and monitor app performance, the App processes the following data:

  • Onboarding Profile Information: During setup, the App requests a child's first name, age, and gender. This is used solely to select custom avatars, personalize dashboard greetings, and adjust learning difficulty.
  • Firebase Analytics & Diagnostics: The App utilizes Google Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics to measure game engagement, track story progress, and capture crash reports to improve stability.
  • Device Notification Tokens: We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens to deliver push notifications (such as story reminders) directly to the device.
  • AdMob Identifiers: The Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) processes necessary device identifiers (like the Advertising ID) and IP addresses solely for operational purposes (e.g. fraud control and frequency capping) as detailed in Section 4.
  • Local Sensory Inputs: Writing/crayon tracing drawings and voiceover playbacks are processed completely locally in transient memory and are never saved or uploaded.

2. COPPA Compliance & Parental Control

Because BolBol is designed for children under 6, we are fully committed to protecting their privacy under the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • Collected profile data (first name, age, gender) is restricted strictly to local customization and targeting non-personalized educational content.
  • This data is never sold, leased, or shared with third-party networks for behavioral advertising or profiling.
  • We disable advertising features in Firebase Analytics to prevent interest-based tracking of children.

3. Camera, Audio, & Storage Permissions

The App may request local system permissions to operate certain elements:

  • Audio Playback: The App uses the device's sound channels to play pre-recorded learning words and ElevenLabs synthesized stories. It does not record audio from the microphone.
  • Local Storage: Temporary assets (like downloaded story audio or custom avatar profiles) may be cached locally on the device's secure internal storage. They are never exported or uploaded.

4. Advertising Policy

The App integrates the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) to display advertisements. To protect child privacy and ensure safety:

  • All ads are requested with the child-directed treatment tag enabled, ensuring Google serves only non-personalized, contextual advertisements.
  • The AdMob SDK may process certain device identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID) and IP addresses solely for operational purposes (e.g., ad frequency capping, fraud prevention, and diagnostics) as permitted under COPPA.
  • No user profiling or interest-based tracking is performed.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the App is completely offline, we recommend reviewing this web page periodically for any modifications. All changes are effective immediately upon posting.
